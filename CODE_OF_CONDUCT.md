# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the KasetPlus project and its community will experience an environment free from harassment and discrimination.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and inclusive of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being patient and kind to others
- Focusing on constructive criticism and feedback
- Respecting the boundaries and privacy of other community members
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Acknowledging and learning from mistakes

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation of any kind
- Discriminatory language or jokes based on race, color, national origin, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or any other protected characteristic
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Spam or repetitive messaging
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project repositories and their issue trackers
- All project communications channels (discussions, comments, pull requests, etc.)
- Project events and meetups
- Any space representing the project or its community

This Code of Conduct also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Community members who violate this Code of Conduct may be subject to actions deemed appropriate by the project maintainers, including:

- A request to stop the offending behavior
- A temporary or permanent ban from project participation
- Reporting to relevant authorities if necessary

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness a violation of this Code of Conduct, please report it by:

1. **Contacting the project maintainers directly** through GitHub
2. **Opening a confidential issue** if you feel comfortable doing so
3. **Reaching out to a trusted community member** who can help facilitate the report

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially. The project maintainers are committed to protecting the identity of reporters.
